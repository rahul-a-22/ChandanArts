CHANDAN'S ARTISTRY

The saree sang around her like a whispered melody, every fold a poem of elegance.
The world faded, replaced by the canvas of her smile, my heart the palette, my favorite spot the frame for a stolen dream.
In my sanctuary of solitude, a tiny heartbeat found its echo, a soft symphony of purrs amid rustling leaves.
A tiny whiskered shadow bloomed into a sunbeam-stealing companion, forever entwining his purrs with the rhythm of my haven.
The familiar haven bloomed anew, mirroring the girl's echo in my heart, both stunning beyond belief.
A furry arrow shot off, leaving me breathless and hopeful, as love's spark leaped between feline paws and her sunlit smile.
Words took flight, hearts in tow, as the haven we shared whispered, "This is where love begins."
